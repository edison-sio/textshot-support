## FAQ

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings → Manage Subscription on your iPhone, or visit the App Store account settings.

**How do I export my screenshot?**
Tap the share button after creating your screenshot, then choose Save to Photos or share directly to any app.

**Is TextShot free to use?**
Yes! TextShot is free to start with access to basic templates. Upgrade to Pro to unlock all templates and remove any limits.

**Do exports include a watermark?**
No, Pro users can export completely watermark-free screenshots.
