**Last updated: March 25, 2026**

Edison Sio ("I", "me", or "my") operates the TextShot app ("App"). This page informs you of my policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use my App.

## Information I Collect

**In-App Purchases**
TextShot uses StoreKit for in-app purchases. I do not collect or store any payment information. All transactions are processed by Apple through the App Store. Your purchase data (subscription status, product IDs) is stored securely by Apple.

**Local Data**
All content you create within TextShot (text, templates, exported images) is stored locally on your device. I do not have access to this data.

**Analytics**
I do not use any third-party analytics services. I may collect anonymous usage statistics (e.g., which templates are used) locally on your device to improve the App, but this data is not transmitted to me.

## Subscriptions

TextShot offers auto-renewing subscriptions:
- **Monthly**: $4.99/month
- **Yearly**: $29.99/year

Subscriptions are managed through Apple. You can cancel or manage your subscription at any time via:
- Settings app on your iPhone → Your name → Subscriptions
- The App Store app on your iPhone

Refunds are handled by Apple. For refund requests, please contact Apple Support or go to reportproblem.apple.com.
